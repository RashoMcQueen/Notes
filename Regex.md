# Regular expresions

## Characters 
- Escape char **\\**
- Any character: **.**
- Digit: **\d**
- Not a digit: **\D**
- Word character: **\w**
- Not a word character: **\W**
- Whitespace: **\s**
- Not whitespace: **\S**
- Word boundary: **\b**
- Not a word boundary: **\B**
- Beginning of a string: **^**
- End of a string: **$**

## Groupings
- Matches characters in brackets: **[ ]**
- Matches characters not in brackets: **[^ ]**
- Either or: **|**
- Capturing group: **( )**

## Quantifiers
- 0 or more: **\***
- 1 or more: **+**
- 0 or 1: **?**
- An exact number of characters: **{ }**
- Range of number of characters: **{Minimum, Maximum}**
